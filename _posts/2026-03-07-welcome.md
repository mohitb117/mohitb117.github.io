Welcome to my new blog! After several years of having my resume as the main content on my GitHub Pages site, I've decided to add a blog section where I can share my thoughts on software engineering, mobile development, and technology.

## Why a Blog?

As a software engineer with over 10 years of experience, I've learned a lot through my journey at companies like Amazon, DoorDash, and INRIX. I thought it would be valuable to share some of these learnings with the community.

## What to Expect

Here are some topics I plan to write about:

- **Android Development** - Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and modern Android architecture
- **Mobile Architecture** - Building scalable apps that serve millions of users
- **Career Growth** - Moving from individual contributor to technical lead
- **Technical Leadership** - Mentoring engineers and building great teams

## Getting Started with Jekyll

This site is built using [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/), which is a static site generator that works perfectly with GitHub Pages. It's simple to use and allows me to write blog posts in Markdown.

Here's a quick example of how easy it is to create a new post:

```markdown
---
layout: post
title: "Your Post Title"
date: 2026-01-01
---

Your content goes here...
```
